# Central Superstore Sales & Profit Dashboard 📊

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales and Profit Performance Dashboard** created using **Microsoft Power BI**.
The dashboard analyzes sales transactions from the **Central Superstore dataset** to provide insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product categories.

The goal of this dashboard is to help users quickly understand business performance and identify trends that support better decision-making.

---

## Key Metrics

The dashboard highlights several important business indicators:

* **Total Sales:** 501.24K
* **Total Profit:** 39.71K
* **Profit Margin:** 7.92%
* **Number of Customers:** 629
* **Number of Orders:** 1K
* **Average Discount:** 24.04%
* **Average Shipping Days:** 4

These metrics provide a quick overview of the company’s overall performance.

## Dashboard Visualizations
![Dashboard](dashboard.png)

### 1. Sales & Profit by Month

A line chart that shows how **sales and profit change across different months** of the year.

### 2. Sales & Profit by Category

A comparison of product categories:

* Furniture
* Office Supplies
* Technology

This visualization helps identify which category generates the most revenue and profit.

### 3. Sales by Segment and Category

A stacked bar chart that analyzes sales for:

* Consumer
* Corporate
* Home Office

### 4. Profit by Discount

A visual analysis showing how **discount levels affect profitability**.

### 5. Product Distribution

Displays how many states sell each product, helping identify product reach and distribution.

### 6. Geographic View

A map visualization showing sales distribution across the **United States**.
